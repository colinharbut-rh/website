Your Data, Your AI, Your Insights

BI Genius is a plug-and-play AI agent creation platform designed to work seamlessly with your existing Power BI Semantic Models and data sources.

Fully deployable in your own Azure environment, BI Genius gives you the power to ask questions, get insights, analysis, and make data-driven decisions like never before.

Why BI Genius?

A White Label BI Agent for You and Clients

Make AI your competitive advantage. BI Genius is a fully configurable AI agent creation platform that integrates directly into your ecosystem, offering a seamless user experience under your brand.

Create and Customize Multiple AI Agents

Create and configure multiple AI agents, each tailored to specific business domains and content areas. Deliver a highly specialized AI experience that aligns with different teams, functions, or industries.

Complete AI Transparency

BI Genius provides complete visibility and transparency into AI-driven logic.  This ensures trust, interpretability, and governance, making AI insights clear, explainable, and actionable.

Easy, Plug-and-Play Setup

No complex integrations, no code required. Simply connect BI Genius to your existing Power BI models and start getting AI-powered insights in minutes. Your AI, your rules.

Fully Deployed in Your Azure Environment

Your data stays completely within your control. Unlike third-party AI solutions that require external hosting, BI Genius operates inside your Azure cloud, ensuring security, privacy, and compliance.

Freedom from Microsoft Fabric Licensing

Unlike Power BI’s built-in features, BI Genius is not bound by Microsoft Fabric’s Co-Pilot capacity licensing. This gives you complete cost control and scalability.

Transparency & Interpretability

Empowering Administrators with AI Visibility

When organizations deploy AI-driven analytics, a key challenge is ensuring that insights are trustworthy, explainable, and actionable.  BI Genius prioritizes clarity, transparency, and control.
The AI-driven platform give administrators a clear view of what’s happening behind the scenes, enabling them to confidently interpret results, answer user questions, and ensure insights are:
  • Understand how AI-generated insights are formed
  • Validate model accuracy and relevance
  • Provide meaningful explanations to end users
  • Maintain governance and compliance over AI-driven decisions
By making AI decision-making transparent and interpretable, BI Genius empowers administrators to not only trust the insights but also defend and explain them.

How BI Genius Enables AI Transparency

Explainable AI Outputs

BI Genius provides administrators with clear breakdowns of how insights are generated, including:

Source Attribution: What data sources contributed to the AI's response.

Decision Path Analysis: A step-by-step view of how the AI arrived at its answer.

Semantic Model Interpretability: How Power BI models influenced the answer.

DAX Statement Formulas: The specific Data Analysis Expression (DAX) formula the model derived to extract the answer.

Administrative Dashboard for AI Oversight

A dedicated AI Logic Dashboard allows administrators to:

Monitor AI activity and track what queries are being processed.

Review past interactions to ensure accuracy and consistency.

Audit logs and history tracking for compliance and accountability.

Customization & Control Over AI Agents

Because BI Genius allows organizations to create and configure multiple AI agents, administrators need control over how these agents interact with users. With BI Genius, they can:

Define knowledge boundaries and restrict AI access to specific datasets.

Apply user & data access controls to govern who can interact with what data within specific AI agents.

Bridging the Gap Between AI and Business Users

AI is only as valuable as the confidence users have in it. With BI Genius:

Business users can ask clarifying questions and get transparent responses.

Administrators can step in with explanations when needed.

Organizations can cultivate trust in AI-driven decision-making.
