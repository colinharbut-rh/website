FAQ

You've got questions.We've got answers.?

How does the 30-day free trial work?
Reporting Hub provides completely free 30-day self-serve free trial. You can deploy the complete Reporting Hub Solution within your Azure environment with no obligation and no credit card required. The 30-day free trial is a self-serve experience.
For organizations that need more hands-on help or customized onboarding, check out our Guided Pilot Program for a more supported evaluation experience.
Are the prices listed in US dollars?
Yes, all prices shown are in US dollars.
Do you offer additional Dev/Test Application Instances?
Yes, Enterprise+ includes a secondary instance for test/development. For all other plans, please contact sales to discuss available options.
Are there term commitments?
There are no term commitments on all monthly plans. Cancel any time without penalty. Annual plans are paid upfront in full.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my subscription?
Yes, you can manage your subscription within the application and upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time as many times as you would like.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards! We also accept bank transfers for annual plans only.
Do you offer discounts for Non-profits?
Yes we do! Contact us directly to find out what discounts are available and for eligibility. Let us know more here!
What happens if I cancel?
You're free to cancel at anytime! When you do, your plan will last until the end of your current billing cycle.
What is your refund policy?
We have a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If for any reason you're not satisfied with your purchase, simply let us know within 30 days and we'll be happy to issue you a full refund.
