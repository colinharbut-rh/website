What's Included in Support?

What IS included with your
Reporting Hub support?
Your Reporting Hub support covers all issues and questions that directly relate to the Reporting Hub software. This includes the software itself, embedded content, integrated Azure resources and integrated web services ONLY as they relate to the Reporting Hub software.
What IS NOT included with your
Reporting Hub support?
General and technical support for the Power BI platform including but not limited to; report development, data architecture & modeling, data integration, refresh schedules, gateway administration etc. General and technical support for the Microsoft Azure platform including but not limited to; Management and administration of Azure resources.
