Jessie G

Product Development & Data Scientist
Jessie is a passionate and skilled Business Intelligence Developer with a knack for transforming data into actionable insights. With a strong background in BI development, Jessie has honed her expertise in identifying business objectives, gathering critical information from stakeholders, and translating these needs into robust, technical solutions.

Throughout her career, Jessie played a pivotal role in developing self-serve reporting and data analysis tools that empowered teams across the organization. She excelled in collaborating with small groups to thoroughly understand business workflows and requirements, ensuring that every BI solution she designed was both practical and innovative.
Jessie's technical proficiency is evident in her ability to extract, combine, and transform data from multiple sources, utilizing Microsoft Power BI to create intuitive, user-friendly dashboards in The Reporting Hub.

Her hands-on experience with data modeling and visualization, coupled with her deep understanding of the Power BI Service, enabled her to deploy BI solutions that significantly enhanced decision-making processes within the company.

Jessie's commitment to excellence in BI development is matched only by her enthusiasm for continuous learning and professional growth. She is always exploring new ways to leverage data for the benefit of her clients and is driven by the desire to make a meaningful impact through her work.
